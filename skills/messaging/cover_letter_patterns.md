# Cover Letter Patterns

Use a cover letter only when it adds specific value. Prefer a short application note when the posting does not require a full letter.

## Short Application Note Pattern

I am interested in the [Role] role because it connects directly to my experience leading product work across [theme], [theme], and [theme]. In recent roles, I have worked with cross-functional teams to modernize complex workflows, improve operational execution, and translate ambiguous business needs into usable product outcomes.

I would bring a practical product leadership style to [Company], with enough technical fluency to work closely with engineering and enough customer orientation to keep the roadmap grounded in real adoption.
