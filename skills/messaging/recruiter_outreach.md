# Recruiter Outreach Patterns

Use concise, confident, human messages that match the resume positioning.

## Pattern

Hi [Name],

I saw the [Role] opening at [Company]. My background lines up strongly with [theme 1], [theme 2], and [theme 3], especially through product leadership across enterprise SaaS, retail operations, and platform modernization.

I would be glad to connect if the team is looking for someone who can step into complex product areas and drive execution with engineering, design, customers, and business stakeholders.

Best,
[Candidate Name]
