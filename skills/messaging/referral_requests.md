# Referral Request Patterns

Referral requests should be easy to say yes to and should include a concise fit summary.

## Pattern

Hi [Name],

I am applying for [Role] at [Company] and thought of you because [connection/context]. The role lines up well with my experience in [theme], [theme], and [theme].

Would you be comfortable referring me, or pointing me to the right person if someone else owns this area?

Best,
[Candidate Name]
