# RPrep Core

Use this file for reusable operating notes that apply across all resume and job application projects.

## Defaults

- Optimize for recruiter shortlist likelihood, hiring manager credibility, defensibility, ATS compatibility, and visual readability, in that order.
- Keep final user-facing writing free of em dashes.
- Keep major claims classified as confirmed, reasonable reframe, strategic extrapolation, high-risk stretch, or do not use.
- Do not include strategic extrapolation or high-risk stretch claims in final artifacts unless the active user approves them.
