# Application Assessment - Left Field Labs Senior AI Technical Product Manager

## Positioning Strategy

Position Raghav as a hands-on senior technical product manager for AI-enabled and workflow-heavy B2B SaaS platforms. The strongest narrative is that he has already done the core motion Left Field Labs needs: turn complex operational domains into clear workflows, technical requirements, live stakeholder prototypes, and launchable product experiences.

The resume should not claim direct insurance expertise. It should use Upshop for compliance-adjacent store operations and use 360insights for claims workflows and financially driven enterprise SaaS, without overstating either as insurance compliance.

## Why This Should Work

- The JD heavily values AI-assisted product operations, detailed documentation, live prototyping, and developer-ready tickets. KWI directly supports this.
- The role needs simplification of dense B2B workflows. KWI, Upshop, and 360insights all support this.
- The role asks for behavior-aware product design. Raghav approved direct behavioral workflow framing based on his experience.
- The role needs technical product fluency. The resume includes APIs, technical requirements, architecture tradeoffs, event-driven workflows, and integration-heavy platforms.
- The role needs direct discovery. Upshop and KWI support customer and end-user discovery.
- The role needs applied AI, not data science ownership. EdgeVerve and Upshop support AI/ML product credibility without positioning Raghav as an ML engineer.
