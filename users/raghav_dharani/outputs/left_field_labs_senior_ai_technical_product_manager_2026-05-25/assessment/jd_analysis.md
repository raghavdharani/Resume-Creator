# JD Analysis - Left Field Labs Senior AI Technical Product Manager

## JD Summary

Senior AI Technical Product Manager role owning the illumend initiative, an AI-first platform focused on simplifying insurance compliance and risk management for non-insurance professionals. The role needs a high-velocity, hands-on technical product leader who can use AI tools deeply, create development-ready product artifacts, conduct rapid prototyping, and translate complex B2B workflows into simple user experiences.

## Must-Have Requirements

- Senior technical product management experience
- AI-first product workflow using tools such as Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, or similar
- Detailed PRDs, documentation, roadmap planning, and developer ticketing
- Rapid or live prototyping
- Direct customer discovery
- Strong user empathy and UX simplification
- Ability to discuss technical architecture and engineering tradeoffs
- Startup-style ownership and velocity
- Product strategy plus hands-on execution

## Nice-To-Have Requirements

- Insurance, compliance, risk management, or regulated workflow context
- Product-led growth, behavioral psychology, activation, engagement, or habit formation
- Executive-facing product ownership
- New platform or new product lane creation

## Seniority Signal

Senior individual contributor with strong ownership. The role is not purely strategic and not people-management led. It is a "strong doer" role with CEO visibility.

## Domain Signal

AI-first B2B SaaS, insurance compliance, risk management, product innovation, applied AI, workflow simplification, technical product delivery.

## Recruiter Screen Risks

- Resume must show hands-on technical product execution, not only leadership.
- AI fluency must be concrete and current.
- Lack of direct insurance compliance experience may be a gap.
- Technical background expectations may be high.
- Prototyping and detailed ticketing must be credible.

## Hiring Manager Risks

- Must prove Raghav can simplify complex B2B workflows into intuitive UX.
- Must demonstrate direct customer discovery and user empathy.
- Must show comfort with architecture and engineering execution conversations.
- Must avoid unsupported claims around AI prototyping or insurance expertise.

## Initial Positioning Recommendation

Position Raghav as a senior technical product leader who has modernized complex operational platforms, translated dense workflows into clear product requirements, partnered closely with engineering, and used AI or AI-adjacent product patterns in practical operating contexts.

Primary evidence areas to inspect from source resumes:

- KWI: platform modernization, technical specs, legacy workflow simplification, AI-assisted delivery if supported.
- Upshop: AI/ML-assisted ordering, retail operations workflows, mobile-first UX, workflow simplification.
- 360insights: SaaS platform, claims workflows, data and integrations, enterprise process automation.
- EdgeVerve: AI/ML, RPA, chatbot, automation product experience.
