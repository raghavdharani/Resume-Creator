# Credibility Audit - Left Field Labs Senior AI Technical Product Manager

## Summary

Credibility Score: 92/100

Overall assessment: Strong and more interview-defensible after revisions. The resume now avoids unsupported mature-launch and adoption claims for KWI, presents AI-assisted delivery as a product practice rather than deep AI engineering, and uses page two for artifact-specific technical product proof instead of filler.

## Key Credibility Fixes Applied

- Replaced the KWI launch/adoption bullet with a current-tense product discovery, requirements, and prototype-validation bullet.
- Removed KWI percentage claims for client migration, transfer-error reduction, requirement clarification, and rework.
- Strengthened KWI modernization ownership with purchasing, item maintenance, store-transfer workflows, legacy constraints, API dependencies, and delivery sequencing.
- Replaced broad behavioral language in Core Strengths with `Workflow Adoption Design`.
- Rebuilt the AI Prototyping and Technical Practice section around concrete artifacts: PRDs, user flows, acceptance criteria, edge-case matrices, Jira-ready stories, prototype screens, API assumptions, and stakeholder walkthroughs.
